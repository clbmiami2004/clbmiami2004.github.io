Features

Designed for meeting overload.

Built for people who need a searchable memory of meetings without making recording hidden or confusing.

Manual Recording

Start and stop recording yourself, with a visible recording state and a privacy notice before capture.

Searchable Transcript

Review transcript text, search within a meeting, and follow along while the audio plays.

Summary and Tasks

Create a meeting summary and candidate action items for review, editing, and completion tracking.

Privacy

Local-first by design.

Meeting audio and notes are designed to stay under the user’s control. Optional sync can be enabled by the user when desired.

Clear recording intent

The app is for personal productivity and does not support covert or always-on background recording.

User-controlled storage

Meetings, transcripts, summaries, and tasks are stored as app data. Users can delete meetings when they no longer need them.
