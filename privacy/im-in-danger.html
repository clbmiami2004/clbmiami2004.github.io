Privacy Policy

I'm in Danger

Effective date: June 1, 2026

I'm in Danger is a personal safety app that helps users alert trusted people, such as parents, friends, or family members, when they may be in danger and choose to share their GPS location.

Information the app may handle

The app may handle the following information when you use its safety features:

  • Location information, when you choose to use location-based safety features.
  • Trusted contact information that you provide for alerting others.
  • Safety alert content or status needed to send danger notifications.
  • Basic app settings used to support the app experience.

Location sharing

Location sharing is intended for safety scenarios where you choose to let trusted people know where you are. You should only share your location with people you trust. Device location access is controlled by Apple system permissions and your device settings.

Service providers

The app may use trusted service providers, such as live location or messaging infrastructure, to deliver safety alerts and location-sharing features. These services should only receive the information needed to provide the feature you choose to use.

Trusted contacts and alerts

The app may use contact or communication information you provide so danger alerts can be sent to the people you choose. You are responsible for choosing appropriate recipients and keeping those contact details current.

Data sharing

The app does not sell your personal data. The app does not use your safety information for advertising. Information may be shared when you choose to send a danger alert, share your location, or use device features required to contact trusted people.

Permissions

The app may request access to:

  • Location: to share your GPS location with trusted people when you choose to use that feature.
  • Contacts or communication features: to help send alerts to the people you select.
  • Notifications: to provide app status or safety-related updates where supported.

Emergency use

I'm in Danger is not a replacement for emergency services. If you are in immediate danger, contact local emergency services when it is safe and possible to do so.

Children’s privacy

The app is intended to be used with appropriate permission, supervision, and legal basis, especially if used by or for minors.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as app features evolve. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
