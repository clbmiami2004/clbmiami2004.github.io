Privacy Policy

My Meeting Transcriber

Effective date: June 1, 2026

My Meeting Transcriber is designed as a personal productivity app for recording meetings after explicit user action, creating transcripts, summarizing meeting content, and helping users manage action items.

Information the app handles

The app may handle the following information when you choose to use its features:

  • Meeting audio recordings that you manually create or import.
  • Transcript text generated from those recordings.
  • Meeting summaries, speaker labels, translations, and action items.
  • Basic app settings, such as sync preference and selected transcript language.

Recording notice and consent

Recording starts only after you take explicit action in the app. You are responsible for providing notice or obtaining consent where required by law, policy, or meeting rules. The app is not intended for covert recording or always-on background surveillance.

Local storage

Meeting data is designed to be stored as app-managed data on your device. This can include audio files, transcript text, meeting summaries, and tasks. You can delete meetings from inside the app when you no longer need them.

iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud sync, meeting data may sync through your Apple iCloud account so it can be available on your other Apple devices. iCloud syncing is controlled by Apple and your iCloud account settings.

Speech recognition and intelligence features

The app prioritizes Apple on-device transcription and local processing where available. Some Apple system features may require Apple-managed language assets or device support. If optional cloud processing is added in the future, it will be presented as a user-controlled setting and this policy will be updated.

Data sharing

The app does not sell your personal data. The app does not use meeting content for advertising. Meeting audio, transcripts, summaries, and tasks are not intentionally shared with third parties by the app unless you choose to export, share, or sync that information using system features.

Permissions

The app may request access to:

  • Microphone: to record meeting audio after you start recording.
  • Speech recognition: to transcribe recordings where required by the platform.
  • Files or sharing features: when you import, export, or share recordings.
  • iCloud: when you choose to sync meeting data across devices.

Children’s privacy

My Meeting Transcriber is not directed to children. Do not use the app to record children or other people without the proper permissions and legal basis.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as app features evolve. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact Christian Lorenzo through: github.com/clbmiami2004

Note: This policy is a practical starting point and is not legal advice. You should review it before publishing the app, especially if you add cloud processing, analytics, accounts, or payments.
