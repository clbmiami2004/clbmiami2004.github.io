My Meeting Transcriber

Local-first meeting memory for Apple devices

My Meeting Transcriber is being built around a simple but important idea: meetings create a lot of cognitive load, and people need a reliable way to capture what was said without turning the app into hidden surveillance.

The product focuses on manual recording, clear recording notice, searchable transcripts, audio playback, summaries, action items, and cross-device access across iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch.

  • Apple-first transcription remains the preferred transcription path where supported.
  • Meetings, transcripts, summaries, translations, and tasks are organized per meeting.
  • The app is designed to support local-first use, with optional iCloud sync for users who want it.

I'm in Danger

A direct safety tool for urgent location sharing

I'm in Danger is a safety-focused app designed to help someone quickly let trusted people know that they may be in danger and share GPS location when help or awareness is needed.

The product is intentionally straightforward: the value is speed, clarity, and getting the right information to parents, friends, or family members in a stressful moment.

  • The app centers on user-initiated danger alerts.
  • Location sharing is positioned as a trusted-contact safety feature.
  • The privacy policy clearly explains location handling and service-provider access.
